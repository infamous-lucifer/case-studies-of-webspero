The client

Blume: safe, clean formulations for young adults

A US-based natural skincare and personal care brand built primarily for young adults and teens. Blume tackles specific skin concerns—like acne, hydration, and sensitivity—using safe, clean formulations. Operating in the hyper-competitive online beauty space, the brand faced the same challenge as every growing D2C retailer: acquiring customers profitably despite skyrocketing Google Shopping CPCs and declining Meta ad ROAS.

The challenge

The eCommerce margin squeeze and Shopify technical debt

The paid acquisition squeeze
Rising Google Shopping CPCs and algorithmic volatility on Meta are actively compressing eCommerce profit margins. Relying solely on paid channels creates a fragile revenue stream that vanishes the second the budget is cut.
Shopify technical friction
Shopify's out-of-the-box architecture frequently generates crawl inefficiencies and URL structure issues. This technical debt quietly suppressed rankings for Blume's high-value collection and product pages.
The Page 2 "no-man's land"
Strong product pages were stranded in positions 11–30. Without intent-aligned optimization, they were entirely missing the critical moments when qualified shoppers were actively searching for clean beauty alternatives.
Missing high-intent coverage
The existing keyword footprint captured low-converting informational noise but lacked coverage for the specific, bottom-of-funnel queries that actually drive eCommerce revenue.
Our approach

Striking distance acceleration + Shopify technical repair

01
High-intent striking distance optimization
We audited the product catalog to identify high-converting pages stuck on Page 2. By aggressively rewriting title tags, meta descriptions, and headers to match exact buyer intent for acne and hydration products, we moved existing assets onto Page 1 for rapid revenue impact.
02
Shopify indexing & schema deployment
We resolved Shopify-specific crawl bottlenecks and implemented product structured data (schema markup). This ensured Google could accurately index the catalog and display rich snippets in SERPs, driving higher click-through rates.
03
Topical authority & linking architecture
We restructured internal linking to funnel domain authority directly to the highest-converting product categories. High-quality external links were secured to establish the site as a definitive resource for safe, natural skincare formulations.

Actionable insights

What eCommerce and retail brands can learn from this campaign

Organic is a margin play
In eCommerce, every percentage point of EBITDA matters. Organic traffic doesn't charge per click, making it the highest-margin acquisition channel in the business once scaled.
Striking distance over new content
Moving a product page from position 12 to position 3 drives exponential revenue growth. Optimizing existing "striking distance" pages yields faster ROI than creating net-new blog content.
Fix your Shopify plumbing
Shopify is excellent for conversion but requires custom technical SEO configurations. Untangling canonicals and collection page architecture is mandatory to compete in high-volume retail search.
Paid dependency is a massive risk
An acquisition model built entirely on Meta and Google Ads is fundamentally fragile. A strong organic footprint creates a defensible competitive moat that pays dividends long after the campaign ends.
