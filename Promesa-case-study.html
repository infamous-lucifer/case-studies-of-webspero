The client

Promesa: trend-driven collections for the modern consumer

A US-based online fashion retailer offering a dynamic mix of clothing, footwear, and accessories. From casual everyday wear to formal attire, Promesa caters to a fashion-conscious audience looking for style and affordability. Competing in an apparel market largely dominated by fast fashion conglomerates and expensive influencer campaigns, the brand needed a sustainable acquisition channel to protect their margins.

The challenge

The volatility of fashion paid media and the Page 2 bottleneck

The paid media squeeze
With Meta CPCs fluctuating seasonally and influencer attribution remaining opaque, relying entirely on paid social was compressing margins and creating unsustainable acquisition costs.
Missing the moment of intent
The site had strong inventory but was failing to rank for highly specific, bottom-of-funnel queries. When shoppers were actively searching to buy, the brand was invisible.
The Page 2 "no-man's land"
Key category and product pages were stuck in positions 11–30. Without precise on-page optimization, these valuable assets couldn't break through to Page 1 visibility.
Shopify crawl inefficiencies
Technical debt common to out-of-the-box Shopify setups—such as URL structure issues and crawl bottlenecks—was actively preventing Google from properly indexing the brand's best collections.
Our approach

Striking distance optimization + Shopify architecture repair

01
High-intent striking distance execution
We targeted conversion-ready keywords sitting on Page 2 and 3. By rewriting title tags, headers, and meta descriptions to perfectly match the exact apparel searches consumers use when ready to buy, we secured rapid Page 1 wins.
02
Technical indexing & rich snippets
We resolved Shopify crawl inefficiencies and implemented product schema markup. This ensured the entire seasonal catalog was indexed correctly and qualified for highly visual SERP rich results, driving up click-through rates.
03
Content authority & internal equity
We restructured the site's internal linking architecture so that existing domain authority flowed efficiently to the most profitable collection pages. This consolidated ranking power without the immediate need for aggressive external link building.

Traffic surged rapidly over a tight three-month window. By capturing over 1,800 new keywords and pushing high-intent apparel queries onto Page 1, Promesa significantly broadened its market share.

Search captures consumers at the highest-intent moment in the entire purchase journey. By moving conversion-ready keywords out of Page 2 obscurity, Promesa built a compounding revenue asset insulated from volatile seasonal ad costs.
Actionable insights

What fashion and lifestyle brands can learn from this campaign

Search intent beats social discovery
Social media generates awareness, but search captures intent. When someone searches for a specific clothing category, they have already decided to buy—they are just deciding where.
Striking distance is pure margin
Pushing a category page from position 12 to 3 drives exponential, immediate revenue. Optimizing existing "striking distance" pages is the fastest way to improve retail EBITDA.
Seasonal demand requires lead time
Fashion search trends are locked in months before the selling season actually begins. Brands building their organic presence now capture peak-season traffic without paying peak-season CPCs.
Shopify technical debt is real
Shopify is an incredible conversion engine, but its out-of-the-box architecture frequently causes crawl issues. Resolving this technical debt is a mandatory prerequisite for enterprise-level retail SEO.
